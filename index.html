About Me

Jogendra Sunda is the Founder and Director of Prince Eduhub Group, based in Sikar, Rajasthan. He started the institution in 1997 with just 35 students, and under his visionary leadership, it has grown into one of Rajasthan’s largest and most respected educational groups—comprising schools, colleges, coaching centers, and NDA academies. Coming from a rural background in Shyampura village, Mr. Sunda is known for his discipline-focused and student-centric approach. His leadership emphasizes struggle, self-reliance, and holistic education, aiming not only to produce academic toppers but also responsible citizens. Today, Prince Eduhub serves thousands of students across multiple campuses, consistently delivering top results in RBSE, CBSE, JEE, NEET, NDA, and other competitive exams.
